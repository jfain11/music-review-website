About Us

Madeline

My favorite album is Deadroses by Blackbear. I have a tattoo for this album, and it has been what I’ve turned to when I’m going through a lot for years. I would describe the genre of this album as pop, in a general sense. I love all the uses of the synths throughout this album. My favorite song on Deadroses is a tie between ‘my heart is lost,’ and ‘4u.’ The opening bass in ‘my heart is lost’ has given me chills on countless occasions, and ‘4u’ has helped me move through heartbreak more times than I can remember. While I think Blackbear has a discography with no bad songs, this album is what I have always turned to the most.

Favorite Album: Deadroses

Jacob

My favorite album is "Sublime" by Sublime. I have listened to this album countless times and really enjoy every song. My dad was a huge sublime fan growing up and it was through him that I was introduced to their music at a young age. I would describe their music as a mixture of rock and reggae, and many often refer to them as a ska-punk band. Their style is largely impacted by the fact the band originates from the west coast, specifically in California, which is clear as the music is very beachy and relaxing.

Favorite Album: Sublime
